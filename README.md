# **Super Galactic Age Calculator**

#### By Ted Leary

## Description

Determine a user's age based on solar years

## Specifications

| Specification | Input | Output |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Take in years and convert to seconds | Input: 26 years] | Output 819,936,000 seconds |
| Take in a two dates and determine the difference in seconds. | (April, 26th, 1988) - (April, 20th, 2018)] | Output 946166400 seconds |
| Convert a user's age from earth years into other planet's years | Input 26 earth years | Output 108 Mercury years |
| Determine a user's remaining years to live on a planet | Input 26 years old | Output 53 years remaining until death |
| If the user is beyond the average life expectancy, return how many years they are beyond the expectancy | Input 80 years] | Output 1 year beyond the average human life expectancy |
